**Good news for authors!**

Due to numerous requests from the community, the ACSOS 2025 Organizing Committee has decided to extend the paper submission deadline.

![Deadline Extended](/images/design/pic01.jpg)

## New Deadlines

*   **Abstract Submission**: ~~May 25, 2025~~ -> **June 8, 2025**
*   **Paper Submission**: ~~June 1, 2025~~ -> **June 15, 2025**

This extension applies to all tracks: Research Papers, Experience Papers, and Vision Papers.

## Why Submit?

ACSOS is the premier venue for research on autonomic computing and self-organizing systems. By submitting your work, you get:

1.  **High Visibility**: Your paper will be published in the IEEE Xplore digital library.
2.  **Expert Feedback**: Receive constructive reviews from our rigorous program committee.
3.  **Networking**: Connect with leading researchers and practitioners in the field.

Don't miss this opportunity to share your work with the community. Submit your paper via [EasyChair](https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=acsos2025).

We look forward to reading your contributions!
