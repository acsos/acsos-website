Registration for **ACSOS 2025** is officially OPEN! We are excited to welcome you to Tokyo, Japan, for a week of learning, networking, and collaboration.

![Registration](/images/design/pic05.jpg)

## Registration Rates

We offer several registration tiers to accommodate different needs. **Early Bird** rates are available until **August 31, 2025**.

| Category | Early Bird (ends Aug 31) | Regular (starts Sep 1) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **IEEE Member** | $600 | $750 |
| **Non-Member** | $750 | $900 |
| **Student Member** | $300 | $400 |
| **Student Non-Member** | $400 | $500 |

### What's Included?

*   Access to all main conference sessions (keynotes, paper presentations, panels).
*   Access to all workshops and tutorials.
*   Conference proceedings (digital).
*   Welcome reception and conference banquet.
*   Daily coffee breaks and lunches.

## How to Register

Please visit our [Registration Page](https://registration.acsos.org) to secure your spot.

> **Note for Authors**: At least one author of each accepted paper must register at the full (non-student) rate by the camera-ready deadline to ensure the paper is included in the proceedings.

## Visa Information

If you require a visa letter, please indicate this during the registration process. We recommend applying for your visa as early as possible.

See you in Tokyo!
