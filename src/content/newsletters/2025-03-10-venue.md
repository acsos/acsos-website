## Exploring Tokyo

Tokyo is a vibrant city that blends the ultramodern and the traditional. While you are here, take some time to explore:
*   **Senso-ji Temple**: Tokyo's oldest temple.
*   **Akihabara**: The center of electronics and anime culture.
*   **Imperial Palace**: The primary residence of the Emperor of Japan.
