We are proud to announce the recipients of the **Best Paper Awards** for ACSOS 2024! The selection committee had a difficult task choosing from so many excellent submissions.

![Awards Ceremony](/images/design/pic02.jpg)

## Best Paper Award

**"Self-Adaptive Microservices in the Edge-Cloud Continuum"**
*   *Authors*: Alice Smith, Bob Jones, and Charlie Brown
*   *Affiliation*: University of Future Systems

This paper proposes a novel approach for dynamic placement of microservices, optimizing for both latency and energy consumption.

## Best Student Paper Award

**"Reinforcement Learning for Autonomous Drone Swarms"**
*   *Authors*: David White (Student) and Eve Black
*   *Affiliation*: Institute of Robotics

David White presented an impressive study on using multi-agent reinforcement learning to coordinate drone swarms in search and rescue missions.

## Best Artifact Award

**"OpenSelf: A Framework for Reproducible Self-Adaptation Research"**
*   *Authors*: Frank Green and Grace Blue
*   *Affiliation*: Open Science Lab

The committee recognized the significant contribution of this open-source framework to the community.

Congratulations to all the winners! You can find the full list of past awards on our [Awards](/awards) page.
