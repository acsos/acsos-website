The **Preliminary Program** for ACSOS 2025 is now live! We have curated an exciting schedule packed with high-quality research presentations, inspiring keynotes, and interactive sessions.

![Program](/images/design/pic06.jpg)

## Program Highlights

Here is a sneak peek at what you can expect:

*   **3 Keynote Speeches**: From world-renowned experts in the field.
*   **12 Technical Sessions**: Covering topics such as "Learning and Adaptation," "Decentralized Coordination," and "Self-Protection."
*   **Poster & Demo Session**: An interactive session to see the latest prototypes and early-stage work.
*   **Doctoral Symposium**: A dedicated track for PhD students to receive feedback on their research.
*   **Social Event**: A traditional Japanese dinner cruise on Tokyo Bay!

## Schedule at a Glance

*   **Day 1 (Sept 29)**: Workshops and Tutorials
*   **Day 2 (Sept 30)**: Opening, Keynote 1, Technical Sessions, Welcome Reception
*   **Day 3 (Oct 1)**: Keynote 2, Technical Sessions, Poster/Demo Session
*   **Day 4 (Oct 2)**: Keynote 3, Technical Sessions, Banquet
*   **Day 5 (Oct 3)**: Technical Sessions, Closing Ceremony, Town Hall Meeting

You can view the detailed program on our [Program Page](/program). Please note that the schedule is subject to minor changes.
