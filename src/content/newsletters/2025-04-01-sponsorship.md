ACSOS 2025 offers a unique opportunity for organizations to showcase their technology, products, and services to a highly qualified audience of researchers and practitioners in autonomic computing and self-organizing systems.

![Sponsorship](/images/design/pic03.jpg)

## Why Sponsor ACSOS?

*   **Brand Visibility**: Get your logo on the conference website, program, and signage.
*   **Talent Acquisition**: Connect with top students and researchers for recruitment.
*   **Networking**: Engage with industry leaders and academic pioneers.
*   **Product Demo**: Showcase your latest tools and platforms in the demo session.

## Sponsorship Packages

We offer several tiers of sponsorship to fit your budget and goals:

### Platinum Sponsor ($5,000)
*   Large logo on website and materials.
*   3 complimentary registrations.
*   Exhibit booth space.
*   5-minute presentation during the opening ceremony.

### Gold Sponsor ($3,000)
*   Medium logo on website and materials.
*   2 complimentary registrations.
*   Exhibit booth space.

### Silver Sponsor ($1,500)
*   Small logo on website and materials.
*   1 complimentary registration.

### Bronze Sponsor ($750)
*   Logo on website.

For more details or to customize a package, please contact our Sponsorship Chair at `sponsors@acsos.org`.
