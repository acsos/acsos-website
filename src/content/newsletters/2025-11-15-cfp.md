The **IEEE International Conference on Autonomic Computing and Self-Organizing Systems (ACSOS 2026)** is pleased to announce that the Call for Papers is now open!

![Call for Papers](/images/design/pic02.jpg)

We invite you to submit your latest research results, ideas, and experiences in all areas of autonomic computing, self-adaptation, and self-organization.

## Important Dates

| Event | Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Abstract Submission** | March 15, 2026 |
| **Paper Submission** | March 22, 2026 |
| **Notification** | May 15, 2026 |
| **Camera Ready** | June 15, 2026 |

## Topics of Interest

We are soliciting contributions in (but not limited to) the following areas:

*   **Autonomic Computing**: Self-managing systems, self-healing, self-optimization.
*   **Self-Organization**: Emergent behavior, swarm intelligence, decentralized control.
*   **Self-Adaptation**: Adaptive software, feedback loops, runtime models.
*   **Cloud and Edge Computing**: Resource management, elasticity, energy efficiency.
*   **Cyber-Physical Systems**: Smart cities, autonomous vehicles, industrial IoT.
*   **AI for Systems**: Machine learning for system management and optimization.

## Submission Guidelines

Papers must be submitted via the conference submission system. All submissions must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.

*   **Full Papers**: Up to 10 pages (excluding references).
*   **Short Papers**: Up to 6 pages (excluding references).

For more details, please visit the [Call for Papers](/cfp) page.

We look forward to receiving your submissions and seeing you at ACSOS 2026!
