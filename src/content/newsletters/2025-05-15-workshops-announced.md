We invite proposals for **workshops** to be held in conjunction with ACSOS 2025. Workshops provide a forum for small groups of researchers to discuss specific topics in depth, often on emerging or specialized areas relevant to the conference.

![Workshops](/images/design/pic02.jpg)

## Proposal Requirements

Workshop proposals should be no more than 4 pages and include:

1.  **Title and Acronym** of the workshop.
2.  **Organizers**: Names, affiliations, and contact information.
3.  **Theme and Topics**: A description of the workshop's focus and its relevance to ACSOS.
4.  **Format**: Planned activities (e.g., keynotes, paper presentations, breakout sessions).
5.  **Expected Interest**: Estimated number of submissions and attendees.
6.  **History**: If the workshop has been held before, provide details on previous editions.

## Selection Criteria

Proposals will be evaluated based on:
*   Relevance to the ACSOS community.
*   Potential to attract attendees.
*   Quality of the organizing team.
*   Diversity of topics covered by the conference workshops.

## Important Dates

*   **Proposal Submission**: July 1, 2025
*   **Notification of Acceptance**: July 15, 2025

Please send your proposals to the Workshop Chairs at `workshops@acsos.org`.

We look forward to your innovative ideas!
