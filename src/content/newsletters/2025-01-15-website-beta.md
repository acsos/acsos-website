Welcome to the **Beta Launch** of the new ACSOS website!

![Beta Launch](/images/design/pic06.jpg)

We have been working behind the scenes to modernize our web presence. This beta version is the first step towards a more dynamic and user-friendly site.

## What to Expect

*   **New Design**: A fresh look and feel.
*   **Improved Structure**: Easier access to conference series, awards, and leadership info.
*   **Mobile Friendly**: Optimized for phones and tablets.

## We Need Your Feedback!

Since this is a beta release, you might encounter some bugs or missing content. We would appreciate your help in identifying these issues.

Please report any problems to `webmaster@acsos.org` or open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/acsos/website).

Thank you for your patience and support as we improve our digital home.
