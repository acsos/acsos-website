Workshops are an integral part of the ACSOS experience, providing a venue for focused discussions on specific topics and emerging research areas. We are pleased to announce the accepted workshops for ACSOS 2025!

![Workshops](/images/design/pic04.jpg)

## Accepted Workshops

We have a diverse set of workshops this year, covering a wide range of interests:

### 1. SISSY: Workshop on Self-Improving Systems Integration
*   **Organizers**: Alice Johnson, Bob Williams
*   **Description**: This workshop focuses on systems that can learn from their own operation and improve their performance over time. It bridges the gap between self-adaptation and machine learning.

### 2. FAS: Foundations of Autonomic Systems
*   **Organizers**: Charlie Brown, Diana Prince
*   **Description**: FAS explores the theoretical underpinnings of autonomic computing. Topics include formal methods, control theory, and rigorous modeling of self-adaptive systems.

### 3. Edgeware: Self-Managing Edge Computing
*   **Organizers**: Eve Adams, Frank Castle
*   **Description**: As computing moves to the edge, management complexity increases. This workshop addresses the challenges of deploying and managing self-organizing applications in edge environments.

## Participation

All workshops will be held on **September 29th, 2025**, the day before the main conference begins.

*   **Submission Deadline**: Please check individual workshop websites for submission deadlines.
*   **Registration**: Workshop registration is included with the main conference registration.

We encourage you to submit your work to these workshops and join the discussion!
