**ACSOS 2024** has come to a close, and what a fantastic week it was! Held in the historic city of Washington, D.C., the conference brought together over 200 attendees from around the world.

![ACSOS 2024 Group Photo](/images/design/pic03.jpg)

## Highlights

*   **Keynotes**: We heard inspiring talks on "Ethical AI" and "Biological Computing."
*   **Workshops**: The workshops on Monday were well-attended and sparked lively debates.
*   **Social Event**: The dinner at the National Air and Space Museum was a highlight for many.

## By the Numbers

*   **150** Submissions
*   **35** Accepted Papers (23% acceptance rate)
*   **5** Workshops
*   **2** Tutorials
